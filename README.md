Stargazing_SQ5

Group Members: Cameron Emsley-Drummond, cemsleyd, 21078179

Description: The user controls a slow moving camera across a field during nighttime, populated with cows, trees, and shiny coins

Setup and Interaction Instructions: Player uses W to look up, D to look right, A to look left, and S to look down. The player uses the mouse to click on coins, adding to score. Press R to reset score and camera alignment. Press H to hide HUD.
